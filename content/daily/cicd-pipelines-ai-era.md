**The book *Accelerate* studied thousands of teams and found something surprising:**

The best teams deploy **more often**, not less. Multiple times per day.

Counterintuitive, right? But here's the logic:

- **Smaller changes = smaller risks.** A week's worth of code is a haystack. One commit is a needle.
- **Faster feedback = faster fixes.** Find a bug Monday, not next month.
- **Less stress = happier developers.** Nobody dreads the deploy when it's just another commit.

**The compound effect is massive:**

Teams with automated pipelines report reduced lead times, higher deployment frequency, and fewer production incidents. They're not working harder. They're just not fighting their own tools anymore.
