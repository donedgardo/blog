So what is?

**Clarity.**

Here's a question I ask every team I coach:

*"How will you know if you did a good job?"*

Simple, right? But watch what happens when you ask it. Blank stares. Nervous laughter. "Well, the ticket said..."

That's the problem. Teams ship features without knowing what success looks like. They hit deadlines but miss outcomes. They're busy, but are they effective?

And here's the thing: **AI makes this worse, not better.**

When generating code is instant, you can build the wrong thing faster than ever. Speed without direction is just faster chaos.
