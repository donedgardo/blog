About Me

As a seasoned software developer with over a decade of experience, I've dedicated my career to elevating the craft of software development. Based in Puerto Rico, I founded and lead a software agency focused on improving the industry's agility and quality of work through modern development practices.

My expertise spans from low-level systems programming in Rust to building scalable web applications. I'm particularly passionate about Test-Driven Development (TDD), clean architecture principles, and exploring cutting-edge technologies like game engines and ray tracing.

I believe in the power of mentorship and knowledge sharing. Throughout my career, I've mentored hundreds of developers, helping them adopt better practices and advance their skills. I regularly write about software development on my blog, sharing insights from real-world projects and exploring innovative approaches to common challenges.

When I'm not building software or mentoring developers, you'll find me exploring the latest developments in game development, working on my ray tracing engine, or contributing to the open-source community.

Development Philosophy

🧪
Test-Driven Development

I advocate for TDD as a fundamental practice that leads to more modular, maintainable, and reliable code. By writing tests first, we ensure code behaves as expected and can be confidently modified.

🏗️
Clean Architecture

I believe in creating clear boundaries between business rules and implementation details, enabling systems that are independent of frameworks, databases, and external agencies.

👥
Collaborative Development

Through pair programming, mob programming, and fostering Westrum organizational culture, I promote collaborative environments that accelerate learning and improve code quality.

🚀
Continuous Improvement

I'm committed to continuous learning and improvement, whether it's exploring new technologies like Rust and Bevy, or finding better ways to solve existing problems.
