About Me

As a seasoned software developer with over a decade of experience, I've dedicated my career to elevating the craft of software development. Based in Puerto Rico, I founded and lead a software agency focused on improving the industry's agility and quality of work through modern development practices.

My expertise spans from low-level systems programming in Rust to building scalable web applications. I'm particularly passionate about Test-Driven Development (TDD), clean architecture principles, and exploring cutting-edge technologies like game engines and ray tracing.

I believe in the power of mentorship and knowledge sharing. Throughout my career, I've mentored hundreds of developers, helping them adopt better practices and advance their skills. I regularly write about software development on my blog, sharing insights from real-world projects and exploring innovative approaches to common challenges.

When I'm not building software or mentoring developers, you'll find me exploring the latest developments in game development, working on my ray tracing engine, or rescuing cats.
